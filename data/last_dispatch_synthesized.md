# Sovereign Dispatch: The Architecture of Agency

**Subject: The Shift to Determinism and the Rise of Sovereign Intelligence**

The era of "vibes-based" AI is ending. We are entering the age of the Specification.

### 01. Deterministic Agency
The transition from *Prompting* to *Specifying* is the most critical shift in agentic architecture. Logic's new spec-driven model recognizes that for AI to handle production-grade infrastructure, it must move away from stochastic guessing. A "Spec" is not a request for an output; it is a definition of a system. Determinism is the only path to trust.

### 02. The Capability Economy
We are witnessing the birth of the Agentic App Store. The value is shifting from the "How" (prompt engineering) to the "What" (executable capabilities). When skills are packaged as verified, transferable assets—defined by rigorous patterns like `SKILL.md`—we move from an economy of attention to an economy of intent. Stop selling the prompt; sell the outcome.

### 03. Strategic Sovereignty
AI is no longer just a tool for productivity; it is a pillar of national security. The UK's £500M investment in Sovereign AI is a declaration of independence from the API-dependency of Silicon Valley. Owning the compute, the data, and the models is the 21st-century equivalent of the space race.

### 04. The Death of the Context Tax
Orchestration is the final frontier. The integration of OpenAI Symphony with Linear signals the transition of the AI from a "coding assistant" to a "Project Manager." By owning the ticket, the AI removes the friction of data movement. The endgame is an end-to-end resolution of complex issues with zero human intervention.

**The signal is clear: The primitive is no longer the prompt. It is the spec, the capability, and the sovereign stack.**
